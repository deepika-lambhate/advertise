FAQ

What should be the number of pages in my Ad?

Number of pages allowed in Ad is one, two, three or four.

Can I upload video Ad?

Yes, but you need to take special permission from AdZeal team to upload Video Ad. Write to AdZeal to upload Video Ad.

I don’t have an Image Ad right now; can I create it on AdZeal?

Yes. You can create an Image Ad in “Ad Maker” . All the tools of Ad creation is provided in Ad Maker in user friendly manner.

What are the various filters available to select my target population?

Following filters are available: Age, Gender, Occupation, Locality and vehicle used

When would Ad Watcher see my Ad?

Ads are visible to Ad Watcher on his/her “Ad Galaxy” after 7:00 AM next day.

Why PPW is more effective than the PPC(Pay Per click)?

In PPC, you are asked to pay for just a click. Whereas in PPW user is asked to interact well with Ad for a specific amount of time (10 seconds per page)and he is asked some questions based on the Ad.
This two way communication makes PPW more effective than the PPC.

How many questions can I ask to Ad Watchers?

For any length of Ad AdZeal asks three basic questions to Ad Watchers:
  1. Rating of the Ad: Ad Watcher needs to give rating in the range of 1 to 5
  2. Interested? : Ad Watcher needs to say ‘yes’ or ‘no’
  3. Name of the Brand: Ad Watcher needs to write your brand name
Apart from these three basic questions, Advertiser also can ask following no. of questions to Ad Watchers:
  1. One Page Ad: No extra questions allowed
  2. Two Page Ad: 1 extra question allowed
  3. Three Page Ad: 2 extra questions allowed
  4. Four Page Ad: 3 extra questions allowed
  5. Video Ad: 1 question per 20 seconds of Ad.

By what time would I get confirmation that whether my Ad would be released next day or not?

You would get this confirmation by 12:00AM.

Are the profiles of the Ad watchers genuine?

Yes, We regularly check genuineness of user’s profile through our “AdZeal Authorized Centers”, where user would be asked to collect their Cash Incentive from. We would also use other means to check genuineness of user profile.

By what time of the day should I upload my Ad?
